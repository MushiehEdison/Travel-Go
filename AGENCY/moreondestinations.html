Discover Douala

Douala, the vibrant economic capital of Cameroon, is known for its bustling markets, stunning waterfront, and rich cultural heritage. It's a city that never sleeps, offering a mix of modern attractions and traditional charm.

Top Attractions

  • Marche des Fleurs: A colorful flower market.
  • Douala Maritime Museum: Learn about the city's maritime history.
  • La Nouvelle Liberté: An iconic sculpture symbolizing freedom.
  • Plage de Youpwe: A beautiful beach to relax and unwind.

Local Cuisine

Experience the culinary delights of Douala, from street food to fine dining. Don't miss out on trying Ndolé, a local dish made with bitter leaves, and grilled fish by the waterfront.

Accommodations

Douala offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget. From luxury hotels to cozy guesthouses, you'll find the perfect place to stay.
